Scenario:

A hacker has breached the National Forensic Science University Dharwad network and is attempting to steal sensitive research data. As a cybersecurity forensic investigator, your mission is to trace the hacker's digital footprints and identify them before they escape with the data.

  5. Complete all puzzles to catch the hacker!
